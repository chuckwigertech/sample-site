Dear Neighbors,
It is an honor to represent you. We are committed to making our area a great place to live, attend school, work, and enjoy parks and trails. We appreciate hearing from you. Whether it’s a town hall meeting, visit, phone call, an email or letter, your ideas matter and help shape our decisions. Please contact us anytime as needed.
We will do our best.
